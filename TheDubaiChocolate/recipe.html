DIY Dubai Chocolate Knafeh: A Simplified Guide



Ingredients

  • 1 pound shredded phyllo dough (kataifi)
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup ground pistachios
  • 1/2 cup chopped dark chocolate
  • 50g of pistachio spread

Equipment

  • Large baking dish or skillet
  • Mixing bowls
  • Spoon
  • Knife
  • Parchment paper
  • Microwave-safe bowl
  • Mold

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Knafeh:

    • Cut the phyllo dough: Cut the shredded phyllo dough into smaller pieces, if desired.
    • Combine with butter: In a large bowl, combine the shredded phyllo dough with the melted butter. Mix until the dough is evenly coated.
    • Toast in a pan: Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the buttered phyllo dough and cook, stirring occasionally, until golden brown and crispy. This may take 5-10 minutes.

  2. Prepare The Spread: Mix the toasted knafeh dough the a jar of pistachio spread.

  3. Melt the Chocolate:

    • Place the chopped dark chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl. Heat in short bursts, stirring between each burst, until melted and smooth.
    • Pour the melted chocolate into the mold, spreading it evenly.

  4. Assemble the Knafeh:

    • Place a layer of the pistachio spread and knafeh in the mold.
    • Top with a layer of melted chocolate once again.

  5. Cool and Freeze:

    • Place the mold in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or until the chocolate is completely set.
    • Once set, remove the knafeh from the mold and freeze until solid.
