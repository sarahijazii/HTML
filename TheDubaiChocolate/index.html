The viral internet sensation known as the Dubai knafeh chocolate bar is a dessert item originally founded by UAE based dessert company Fix Chocolate. Termed as the "Can't get Knafeh of it" bar, it was released back in April of 2021, but only arose in popularity in the spring of 2024.

The chocolate bar consists of creamy milk chocolate filled with sweet and decadent pistachio cream, which is combined with toasted knafeh which is a middle eastern phyllo dough.

The combination of the creamy chocolate with sweetness of the pistachio cream and the crispy texture the toasted knafeh adds creates a combination that addicted the world. The Fix chocolate bar became a global hit which people referred to as “The Dubai Chocolate Bar”.

This chocolate bar went viral all over social media platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, Tiktok and Snapchat. Creators rushed to purchase this bar and followers soon gave into the trend. The trend spread world wide and celebrities and influencers from the United States all the way to South Korea flocked to recreating the chocolate bar and purchasing it from global second hand resellers.
