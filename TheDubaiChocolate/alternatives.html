Alternatives to the Viral Fix Chocolate

The Fix Chocolate Bar has become such a hit sensation that many companies have rushed to recreate and sell the same products while riding the hype waves that have been generated by the viral startup. The internet refers to them as “dupes” originating from the word duplicate. Others have rushed to recreate the recipe in their homes. Hamouda the founder of Fix said “We get messages from people saying, ‘thank you for inspiring us to be creative at home,’ so that’s something that I’m proud of,” she says. You can find the recipe in the DIY section of our website.
