Learn About the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World

Fun Facts:

  • Fact 1: The Great Pyramid of Giza is the oldest of the Seven Wonders and the only one still standing today.
  • Fact 2: The Lighthouse of Alexandria was one of the tallest man-made structures in the world for many centuries.
  • Fact 3: The Hanging Gardens of Babylon may not have even existed, with no definitive archaeological evidence of their location.
  • Fact 4: The Statue of Zeus in Olympia was so large that it nearly filled the entire temple where it stood.
  • Fact 5: The Mausoleum at Halicarnassus was built by Artemisia II in memory of her husband, and it was so grand that its name became synonymous with any grand tomb.
